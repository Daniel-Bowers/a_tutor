Non-Coordinate Geometry ▼

  • circle rules visual reference
  • quadrilateral specificity diagram ▼
    • rules for less-specific shapes apply to more-specific shapes
    • having more rules gives you more ways to solve problems
    • e.g. square rules
      • angles add to 360° (quadrilateral)
      • two sides are parallel (trapezoid; not useful because parallelogram rules include this)
      • two pairs of parallel sides (kite; not useful because rhombus rules include this)
      • two pairs of congruent angles (isosceles trapezoid; not useful because rectangle rules include this)
      • all sides parallel (parallelogram)
      • all angles are right angles (rectangle)
      • all sides equal (rhombus)
